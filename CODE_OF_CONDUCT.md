# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Open CoT is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on ideas, not people.
- Give actionable, evidence-based feedback.
- Support newcomers and explain project context when needed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warning.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may take corrective action as needed.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to maintainers through private channels listed in project governance communications.
